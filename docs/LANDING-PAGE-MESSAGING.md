# Messaging va Cau Truc Chuyen Doi Landing Page MIU SQUARE

Cap nhat: 2026-03-25
Nguoi soan: CMO

## 1. Muc tieu kinh doanh cua landing page

Landing page khong chi de "gioi thieu dich vu". Muc tieu la chuyen doi nhom SME/local business dang chay ads Facebook nhung xu ly lead chua tot thanh 1 cuoc hen tu van hoac 1 yeu cau demo.

Chi so uu tien:

- Tang ty le submit lead form
- Tang ty le click CTA "Nhan tu van" hoac "Dat lich demo"
- Do duoc chi phi moi lead hop le tu cac kenh ads
- Do duoc toc do phan hoi lead va ty le lead duoc handoff

## 2. Chan dung khach hang muc tieu

### Nhom uu tien 1: Chu doanh nghiep nho va local service business

- Dang chay Facebook Ads de lay lead cho bat dong san, giao duc, tham my, F&B, dich vu dia phuong
- Ngan sach ads da co, nhung khau sau click van roi rac
- Thuong nhan lead qua inbox, form, sheet, Zalo, hoac nhan su tu xu ly tay
- Dau hieu dau don:
  - Lead vao nhieu nhung phan hoi cham
  - Sale khong biet uu tien lead nao truoc
  - Khong co dashboard de biet lead den tu dau va rot o dau
  - Kho chung minh ROI marketing cho chu doanh nghiep

### Nhom uu tien 2: Agency service nho dang van hanh lead cho client

- Can mot quy trinh co the lap lai cho nhieu client
- Muon giam phu thuoc vao thao tac tay va nguoi van hanh
- Can ban "automation + minh bach + toc do" thay vi chi ban ads management

### Job-to-be-done

"Khi toi da bo tien chay Facebook Ads, toi can lead duoc thu ve, phan loai va day cho team sale nhanh, de khong mat khach vi cham phan hoi."

## 3. Dinh vi va gia tri cot loi

### Value proposition chot

MIU SQUARE giup doanh nghiep bien lead Facebook thanh pipeline co the do luong va xu ly nhanh, thay vi de lead nam roi trong inbox.

### Loi hua san pham

- Thu lead tap trung tu landing page va Facebook
- Day thong bao va handoff cho sale trong 5 phut
- Co dashboard de theo doi trang thai lead va diem rot

### Khac biet can nhan manh

- Khong chi la website: day la lop chuyen doi + workflow automation
- Khong chi gom lead: con co handoff va tracking den buoc xu ly tiep theo
- Khong chi setup 1 lan: duoc thiet ke de mo rong thanh quy trinh van hanh lap lai

## 4. Messaging framework

### Headline chinh

**Bien lead Facebook thanh pipeline chot sale nhanh, khong con roi trong inbox**

### Subheadline chinh

MIU SQUARE giup SME va local business thu lead, phan loai, thong bao cho sale va theo doi conversion trong mot quy trinh co the do luong.

### CTA chinh

**Dat lich demo quy trinh**

### CTA phu

**Nhan tu van giai phap**

### Proof points can dua len man hinh dau

- Phan hoi lead nhanh hon nho thong bao va handoff tu dong
- Giam that thoat lead do xu ly thu cong va roi rac
- Co dashboard de biet lead den tu dau, ai dang xu ly, va dang rot o dau
- Phu hop voi thin-slice MVP: co the bat dau tu 1 use case ROI ro rang

### Cong thuc thong diep xuyen suot

- Van de: Dang do tien vao ads nhung mat lead o khau follow-up
- He qua: Sale cham, khong co minh bach, khong biet kenh nao hieu qua
- Giai phap: Gom lead, tu dong handoff, theo doi conversion
- Ket qua: Xu ly nhanh hon, ro ROI hon, de scale hon

## 5. Proof va xu ly objection

### Proof points chi tiet

- Quy trinh "lead vao -> phan loai -> thong bao -> theo doi" ro rang, de demo
- Kien truc cho phep mo rong tu landing page sang dashboard va automation flow
- Tap trung vao use case co ROI de do trong giai doan dau, khong om qua nhieu module

### Objection can xu ly trong copy

- "Toi da co nguoi chay ads roi": MIU SQUARE khong thay ads, no sua khau chuyen doi va xu ly lead sau ads
- "Toi da co CRM": MIU SQUARE giai quyet intake, handoff va workflow phan hoi nhanh truoc khi du lieu bi rot
- "Setup co phuc tap khong?": Bat dau tu 1 funnel gon, 1 CTA, 1 quy trinh handoff, roi mo rong sau

## 6. Cau truc noi dung landing page theo funnel

### Section 1: Hero

Muc tieu: lam ro van de, doi tuong, va ket qua.

- Headline chinh
- Subheadline chinh
- 1 CTA chinh, 1 CTA phu
- 3 proof bullets ngan
- Visual goi y cho UI: minh hoa flow lead di tu Facebook/landing page vao dashboard va sale handoff

### Section 2: Van de kinh doanh

Muc tieu: khach hang tu nhan ra no dau cua ho.

- Lead vao nhieu kenh, phan hoi cham
- Sale bo sot lead nong
- Khong biet ads tao lead chat luong hay lead rac
- Chu doanh nghiep khong nhin thay ROI that sau khi da do tien

### Section 3: Cach MIU SQUARE giai quyet

Muc tieu: giai thich co che gia tri, khong sa vao technical jargon.

- Thu lead tap trung
- Phan loai va day thong bao
- Handoff sale nhanh
- Theo doi trang thai va conversion tren dashboard

Format de xuat: 4 cards theo flow.

### Section 4: Ket qua va gia tri nhan duoc

Muc tieu: noi ve outcome, khong chi feature.

- Rut ngan thoi gian phan hoi lead
- Giam that thoat lead trong khau follow-up
- Minh bach hoa quy trinh marketing -> sale
- Tao nen tang de scale ads ma khong tang chaos van hanh

### Section 5: Use case phu hop

Muc tieu: giup khach hang tu xep minh vao dung nhom.

- Bat dong san can xu ly lead nhanh theo du an
- Giao duc can goi lai trong khung gio vang
- Tham my/F&B can chot lich hen, khong de mat lead
- Agency service can van hanh lead cho client co quy trinh

### Section 6: CTA chuyen doi

Muc tieu: ep hanh dong ro rang.

- Tieu de: "Muon biet lead cua ban dang rot o dau?"
- Form ngan hoac nut dat lich demo
- Nhac lai 3 loi ich: nhanh hon, ro hon, de scale hon

### Section 7: FAQ / objection handling

Muc tieu: giam can tro truoc khi submit.

- Phu hop voi loai hinh doanh nghiep nao?
- Co can thay he thong hien tai khong?
- Bat dau tu use case nao de thay ROI som?

## 7. Tracking va event can ho tro tren web

### Muc tieu do luong

- Biet nguon traffic nao tao conversion
- Biet section nao tao click/scroll tot
- Biet form rot o buoc nao
- Tao du lieu cho remarketing va toi uu landing page sau nay

### Event de xuat

| Event | Khi nao ban | Thuoc tinh can luu | Muc dich |
|-------|-------------|--------------------|---------|
| `page_view` | Mo landing page | `source`, `medium`, `campaign`, `content`, `device` | Do traffic vao trang |
| `hero_cta_click` | Click CTA o hero | `cta_type`, `cta_label`, `source`, `campaign` | Do suc hut thong diep dau trang |
| `section_view` | Den cac moc section chinh | `section_id`, `percent_scrolled` | Do muc do consume noi dung |
| `proof_point_click` | Click vao card/chi tiet proof | `proof_id`, `position` | Do proof nao duoc quan tam |
| `use_case_click` | Click vao use case/nganh | `use_case`, `position` | Do phan khuc nao thu hut |
| `lead_form_start` | Focus vao form lan dau | `form_id`, `source`, `campaign` | Do y dinh chuyen doi |
| `lead_form_submit` | Gui form thanh cong | `form_id`, `source`, `campaign`, `use_case`, `company_size` | KPI conversion chinh |
| `lead_form_error` | Submit loi | `form_id`, `field`, `error_type` | Tim diem rot ky thuat/UX |
| `secondary_cta_click` | Click CTA phu | `cta_type`, `cta_label`, `location` | So sanh hero CTA va CTA duoi trang |
| `contact_channel_click` | Click Zalo/phone/email | `channel`, `location` | Do y dinh lien he ngoai form |

### Conversion KPI can theo doi

- Visitor -> lead form start
- Lead form start -> lead form submit
- Hero CTA click-through rate
- Conversion rate theo nguon campaign
- Tyle lead duoc phan hoi trong SLA 5 phut

## 8. Input handoff cho UI Designer

- Hero phai the hien duoc 3 lop thong diep: lead Facebook, xu ly nhanh, do luong duoc
- Uu tien flow/minh hoa quy trinh hon hinh stock chung chung
- CTA chinh phai noi ve demo/quy trinh, khong dung copy mo ho nhu "Tim hieu them"
- Moi section can co 1 mot thong diep chinh, tranh nhet qua nhieu feature
- Proof points nen trinh bay duoi dang cards, timeline, hoac process steps de doc nhanh

## 9. Input handoff cho Product Owner

- MVP landing page nen co 1 page duy nhat voi cac section tren
- Form can toi gian, de uu tien conversion:
  - Ho ten
  - So dien thoai
  - Nganh/ngach kinh doanh
  - Nhu cau hien tai hoac van de gap phai
- Tracking event trong muc 7 can duoc dua vao acceptance criteria frontend
- CTA can co it nhat 2 vi tri: hero va cuoi trang
- Can co trang thai submit thanh cong va log nguon campaign/UTM ngay tu dot 1

## 10. Copy guardrails

- Noi theo ket qua kinh doanh, khong noi theo feature ky thuat
- Tranh tu vung qua enterprise nhu "omnichannel", "digital transformation" neu khong co bang chung
- Uu tien ngon ngu de hieu, sat no dau van hanh
- Nhac lai cum tu "lead Facebook", "phan hoi nhanh", "pipeline do luong duoc" nhat quan xuyen suot
