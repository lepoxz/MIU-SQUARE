# Market Analysis

## Target Market

- **Users**: SME, local service businesses va seller can lead tu Facebook nhung thieu quy trinh xu ly nhanh
- **Problem**: Lead do vao nhieu kenh, bi rot vi phan hoi cham, khong co dashboard tap trung va phu thuoc thao tac tay
- **Market size**: Rong o nhom doanh nghiep nho va agency service tai VN, dac biet trong bat dong san, giao duc, tham my va F&B

## Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Strengths | Weaknesses | Differentiation |
|------------|-----------|------------|-----------------|
| Full-service marketing agency | Quan ly ads va creative tron goi | Van hanh tay, bao cao cham | MIU SQUARE nhan vao automation va toc do handoff |
| CRM tool don le | Quan ly du lieu lead | Khong close loop voi campaign execution | MIU SQUARE gom ca intake + automation + execution view |
| Freelancer setup chatbot | Gia re, trien khai nhanh | Thieu van hanh dai han va dashboard | MIU SQUARE ban quy trinh co the mo rong, khong chi mot bot |

## Positioning

- **Value proposition**: Bien lead Facebook thanh pipeline co the do luong va van hanh duoc, thay vi inbox roi rac
- **Target segment**: Doanh nghiep dang chi ngan sach ads nhung chua co quy trinh follow-up on dinh
- **Key differentiators**: Tich hop workflow automation, dashboard minh bach va kha nang ban giao cho team sale nhanh

## Risks

- Facebook API/chinh sach thay doi anh huong ingestion flow
- Khach hang nho co the nhay cam voi chi phi setup ban dau
- Du lieu campaign phan tan neu client dang dung nhieu cong cu roi rac

## Recommendations

Bat dau bang thin slice tap trung vao mot use case co ROI ro: thu lead Facebook, thong bao sale trong 5 phut, va dashboard theo doi conversion ban dau. Chi khi luong nay on dinh moi mo rong sang content scheduling hoac bao cao nang cao.
